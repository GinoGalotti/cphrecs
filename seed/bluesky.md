# Blue Sky

## 1 - Warming up

Seeing you always makes me beam.

I love how easy it is to spot you in a crowd and, as soon as I realise you are there, I start smiling like a silly man. And that day was no exception. The moment I saw you coming out of the station I started swaying side to side in joy... Although some would say it was due to the ants under my skin from the pre-workout.

I was very excited to share my happy place with you, and the excitement was so obvious you got infected by it. I really cherish my time pretending to be an amateur athlete, and I love having my favourite people with me... You have no idea how nice it felt realising that that's something we could share sometimes! Although some would say it was all a ruse to check you out in that lovely workout set.

You were a bit nervous with the change of place, but I loved seeing you relax every time I got near and reassured you. We were virtually alone, and it was fun to see how different our ways to warm up were, picking different songs, preparing the sets... All covered by jokes and silly comments. We stopped pretending very quickly and grew comfortable goofing and joking around, quickly forgetting that there could be anyone around. Although some would say it was just another excuse to hear you bantering and laughing, I love how direct you can be with your comments.

And we also trained. I saw how freaking strong, able and capable you are, challenging me on many exercises I'd forgotten. While I shared a few things that have worked for me and tried to help with my silly calisthenic tricks, melting when I saw you relax into it and smile the moment you realised I would be your biggest fan. Stern and a bit demanding, but not wasting a second to praise how amazing you are. I wasn't shy of getting close to you either, with the thousand excuses that a workout provides. There is no better spot on a pull-up than gently but firmly holding you on the way up, reminding you how safe and tossable you could be in my arms. Some would say it was all an excuse to see you laugh, sweat and get flustered... Fuck, how you could get even hotter than usual.

But we behaved. There was clear attraction, obvious energy and a few rests where we reminded each other how attractive we looked taking care of ourselves. We laughed and swore. Poked and spotted. And we came out of it with a warm, tingly feeling telling us this is something we would want more of. "You can grab a towel there and have a shower, there is even a tiny sauna. But I live next door, if you want to shower there while I prepare you a little bite to recover". My job is to make you feel always desired, but never pressured, and it would have been a delightful day even if it had finished at that point. But you looked at me and smiled. With that damn smile. "I'm sure I would prefer your shower", you said, dropping the voice, "but I don't know if you would be preparing any food..." Fuck. That smile.

We grabbed two shakes on the way out.
